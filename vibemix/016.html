  The music genre that was a short a short life
  span but huge influence on Jungle which then
  morphed into Drum&Bass. The tracks are from
  1991 to 1994 and contain the essence of what

  hopefully bring some warm memories to those who
  were lucky enough to get involved at the time!

  vocal licks, techno synths and a touch of dub.
